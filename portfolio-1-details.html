The Led Zeppelin Game

This project was my first ever attempt at building a game with JavaScript, JQuery and CSS alone. I had tons of fun creating it and fixing the bugs. The inspiration came from my love for the rock and roll band "Led Zeppelin" and also the joys I have with spending time with friends and opening a cold one.
